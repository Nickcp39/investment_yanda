## 2. Evidence vs Interpretation

### Evidence (what the sources state)

- The anchor quarter pairs a record, sharply-growing Data Center line with still-weak Gaming and Professional Visualization (channel normalization), and a Q2 revenue guide (~$11B) far above the prior quarter (~$7.19B) and above reported consensus.
- The full-year 10-K baseline shows Data Center already on a steep multi-year climb ($6.7B -> $10.6B -> $15.0B) while total revenue was flat because Gaming fell.
- Management framing is explicit that generative AI / LLMs drive the inflection and that supply was being increased for 2H.
- The balance sheet is strong (net debt modestly positive cash position; large cash + marketable securities) and the company carries large forward supply/capacity and cloud-service commitments.
- At the locked as-of price the equity is ~$0.94T, with trailing GAAP P/E ~198x and trailing non-GAAP P/E ~124x (arithmetic from primary EPS; not a judgment).
- A credible competitor product (AMD MI300) was already publicly disclosed before the as-of date.

### Interpretation (deferred — NOT done here)

- Whether this is an exceptional bottleneck worth the optical multiple, or a peak/priced-for-perfection setup, is exactly the future decision question and is intentionally left open.

### Sentiment / Narrative Only (lead, not proof)

- The ~$1T valuation framing and the "trillion-dollar installed base will be accelerated" line are narrative/management framing (S006, S011, S012). Treat as leads; the future analysis must test them against revenue conversion, margins, and competitive durability.
