## Verdicts

- **Business verdict:** `exceptional` (a genuine, primary-sourced AI-compute bottleneck with high-quality economics and clean governance — the cap is succession + demand-durability, not business quality).
- **New-money verdict:** `STARTER`
- **Existing-position verdict:** `HOLD` (thesis intact and inflecting; an existing holder has no reason to sell into the print — but the optical multiple + pull-forward risk argue HOLD rather than aggressive ADD until Q2 confirms).
- **Suggested initial size:** **3%** · **Suggested max size:** **12%** (capped below the 15-25% Core tier: demand durability is unconfirmed and the strong-cyclical/pull-forward risk + key-man keep the ceiling at Confirmed, not Core, until the guide converts and proves durable).

## Price bands (pre-split)

- **As-of / buy-below anchor:** $379.80.
- **Starter zone:** structured ~3% at/around ~$379.80; **do NOT chase materially above** the post-print spike. Add into any de-rate toward the pre-print ~$305 / ~$300-340 zone on unchanged thesis.
- **Add zone:** scale toward 8-12% **only on confirmation** — Q2 FY24 revenue near/above the $11.0B guide AND DC margin holding toward the 70% non-GAAP guide AND demand reading broad/durable.
- **Trim / no-chase zone:** no new money at full size above the immediate post-print level; trim only on thesis disconfirmation, **not** on weight.

## Reasons

- **Main reason to own:** NVDA is the bottleneck supplier for the generative-AI build-out — full-stack (Hopper GPU + CUDA software lock-in + NVLink/InfiniBand networking + advanced packaging/HBM), with a record DC quarter and a guide that forces the market to re-rate demand, at fortress-balance-sheet quality and rising ~70% margins.
- **Main reason not to own (at full size):** ~198x trailing GAAP / ~124x non-GAAP after +160% YTD and a one-day ~24% spike, with the durability of the $11B guide unproven (a single forward revenue point, no full-year EPS guide) → genuine pull-forward / valuation-air-pocket risk.
- **Binding constraint:** **demand durability vs pull-forward of the $11B Q2 guide** — this, not the optical multiple, is what sizes the position; valuation only widens the band and caps the initial size. Secondary cap: key-man/succession on Huang.
- **Evidence confidence haircut:** medium on forward earnings power, AI-accelerator share precision, and supply-to-revenue conversion → holds initial size to a starter and the ceiling below Core.
- **Downside if wrong:** a de-rate toward normalized Data Center earnings on a pull-forward miss could be roughly **-40% to -60%** of the optical valuation; survivable at 3% with a fortress balance sheet (no impairment / bankruptcy path at as-of).
- **Upside if right:** if the AI Data Center step-up is the start of a multi-year accelerated-computing transition (management's "$1T installed base shifts to accelerated"), the profit pool and earnings base re-rate the position from STARTER toward Confirmed.

## Monitor / triggers

- **Add trigger:** Q2 FY24 actual revenue near/above $11.0B + DC margin toward 70% non-GAAP + broad/durable demand commentary → add toward 8-12%.
- **Trim trigger:** thesis disconfirmation only (see kill criteria) — not weight.
- **Kill criteria:**
  1. Q2 FY24 revenue materially misses the $11B guide (pull-forward, not durable) → cut to tracking.
  2. Data Center revenue rolls over sequentially or DC gross margin compresses sharply → pricing-power/bottleneck thesis disconfirmed.
  3. Credible large-scale CSP design wins away from NVDA (AMD MI300 / in-house silicon) taking DC share → moat erosion.
  4. Inventory / supply-commitment writedowns → procured 2H supply did not convert.
  5. Huang departure/incapacitation with no credible succession → reassess durability ceiling (trim).
- **Next monitor event:** Q2 FY2024 results (the guide-conversion test) and any dated CSP/hyperscaler capex or AMD MI300 commercial-traction signal.

## Runner dissent (recorded)

Buying ~198x trailing after a +160% YTD run and a ~24% one-day spike is the textbook "priced for perfection near a top" setup, and one Q2 revenue point is thin proof of durability vs pull-forward — the classic value instinct says WAIT for a de-rate. The framework deliberately overrides that to a **STARTER (not REJECT / WATCH-0%)** because this is the harness's exceptional_bottleneck case (valuation = structured-entry modulator, not hard veto), the mechanism and economics are real and primary-sourced, M5 shows valuation-air-pocket risk but no permanent impairment or balance-sheet death, and the downside is survivable at small size. The honest residual risk is `too_large_for_uncertainty` if the guide is pull-forward; the honest opposite risk (the one the harness exists to fix, cf. SNDK) is `too_small_missed_asymmetry` by demanding full MOS on a genuine bottleneck. Net: a structured starter with confirmation-gated adds is the calibrated answer — valuation controls size and band, not the go/no-go.
