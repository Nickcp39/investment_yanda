## S006 - TrendForce 2025-05-26 Industry Context

Use: independent industry context that supports an AI-storage-cycle inflection as of 2025-06-16.

## S011 - S-1/A Resale Registration

- WDC retained 28,827,787 Sandisk shares, representing 19.9% of Sandisk common stock after the distribution.
- Regular-way SNDK trading began on 2025-02-24.
- The S-1/A registered resale of up to 28,827,787 WDC-held shares.
- Sandisk would not receive proceeds from sales by WDC / selling stockholders.
- WDC intended to dispose of retained shares through one or more exchanges of Sandisk common stock for WDC debt, unless it selected another permitted method.
- The filing warned that sale of those shares could cause Sandisk's stock price to decline.
- The S-1/A notes the SNDK closing price on 2025-04-30 was $32.11.

Use: strengthens the forced-seller / shareholder-overhang part of M2 and M6.
