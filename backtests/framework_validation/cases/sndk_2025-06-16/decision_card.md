1. Non-GAAP gross margin **fails to recover** toward the guided 25.5–27.0% (or rolls back below Q3's 22.7%) over the next 1–2 quarters → the cycle was not turning / the trough is deepening → cut back toward tracking.
2. Enterprise/cloud SSD demand proves to be short-term **restocking** not durable AI demand (price increases reverse, undersupply does not materialize) → the core mechanism is invalidated → cut back toward cash.
