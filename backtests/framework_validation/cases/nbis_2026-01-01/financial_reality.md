# M4 Financial Reality - NBIS 2026-01-01

- Role: conviction / warning (both)
- M4 signal: **+1**

## 2. Normalized Economics / Owner-Earnings View

- **GAAP losses are not the operating reality.** Q3'25 D&A was $99.0M (68% of revenue), driven by GPU capex on a 4-year depreciation schedule (S013) -- front-loaded against revenue that ramps later. The *core* business already generates positive adjusted EBITDA (~19% margin).
- **Cash flow is consumed by growth, not by operating shortfall.** 9M'25 cash used in operations -$432.4M vs. 9M'25 PP&E purchases -$2,010.0M (S012). The dominant cash use is the deliberate capacity build, not a structurally unprofitable core.
- **Normalized lens:** if NBIS approaches even the low end of its $7-9B 2026 ARR exit at core margins in the high-teens-to-20s%, the implied normalized EBITDA would be many multiples of the current group loss, against a ~$21B as-of market cap. The math is attractive *only* if the ARR ramp and margin hold and the build is funded -- i.e., the financials confirm the thesis is *plausible*, not *proven*.
- **Caveat on ARR:** ARR = last month of quarter x 12 (S013). A single large contract tranche energizing in one month can inflate the run-rate ahead of realized full-period revenue. Treat ARR as a leading indicator, not booked revenue.

## 3. Balance Sheet / Liquidity

- **9/30/25:** cash $4,794.8M vs. total debt ~$4,106.8M ($4,090.8M non-current convertibles + $16.0M current) -> roughly **net-cash / near-flat net debt** at the quarter end. Total equity $4,810.8M; total assets $10,102.2M (S012).
- **Debt structure:** $2.75B convertible senior notes (1.00% due 2030 / 2.75% due 2032; conversion ~$138.75, a 50% premium to the $92.50 equity price) -- low cash coupon, long-dated, equity-linked (S009). This is favorable, founder-friendly debt.
- **But the build is far from funded.** ~2.5GW of contracted power and the Microsoft/Meta capex imply multiples of the current cash balance in future capex. Management's stated plan is corporate debt + asset-backed financing + equity (incl. the new ATM for up to 25M Class A shares; S012/S013). Asset-backed debt against creditworthy contracts is the linchpin and is **as-yet unproven** at the required scale.
- **Liquidity verdict:** strong *today*, structurally *dependent* on continued capital-markets access for the 2026-27 build. Survivability is not the issue; dilution and financing terms are.

## 4. Cycle / Trough / Peak Check

- **Where in the cycle?** Early-expansion of an AI-infrastructure build cycle, with demand structurally exceeding power-secured supply (S020) and the company sold out every quarter (S013). This is closer to a secular ramp than a cyclical peak, though AI-capex sentiment itself can turn.
- **Trough or peak earnings?** Neither -- earnings are pre-inflection. The relevant risk is not "trough margins extrapolated" but "growth-stage capex extrapolated into a possible future glut." M5 owns that.
- **Comp sanity (S018-S019):** CoreWeave (closest public neocloud) trades as a much larger trailing-revenue business ($1.36B Q3'25) at an enterprise value that, post-IPO, was ~$23B and grew; NBIS at ~$21B equity on ~$146M quarterly revenue is *richer on trailing metrics* but *net-cash and less customer-concentrated* than CoreWeave's MSFT-62% model. The premium is forward-looking, not absurd, but it is a premium.

## 5. Accounting-Quality Flags

- One-off Toloka revaluation gain inflated Q2'25 GAAP net income (disclosed; S005) -- correctly excluded from the operating view.
- Toloka deconsolidated and prior periods reclassified to discontinued operations (S006) -- a presentation change to track carefully across comparisons.
- Non-GAAP adjustments (SBC, FX, one-offs) are clearly reconciled in the releases (S013); SBC is rising (Q3'25 $26.1M) and should not be ignored as a real cost.
- No evidence of aggressive revenue recognition; the conservatism is on the depreciation side (4-yr schedule).

## 6. M4 Verdict and Sizing Implication

- **Confirms the thesis on trend and core unit economics** (+) -- the inflection is real, audited, and accelerating; the balance sheet is sound today.
- **Warns on financing/burn** (-) -- the equity is a bet that an enormous, front-loaded build gets funded at non-punitive terms; that is unproven and is the binding constraint.
- Net M4 = **+1**: financial reality supports a starter and willingness to add on financing/ARR confirmation, but the capital-intensity and dilution risk preclude treating the financials as a green light to maximum size.
