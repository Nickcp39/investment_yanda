### Per-person life-arc — formation first

**Arkady Volozh** (highest weight — the controller)

*Formation (unchooseable → value formation):*
- **Family & home:** born in **Guryev, Kazakh SSR** (now Atyrau, Kazakhstan) into a **Russian-Jewish family**;
  **father a petroleum geologist, mother a music teacher** (O01, O02). A technical-professional, not wealthy or
  politically connected, household — engineering/quantitative values, minority-outsider position within the USSR.
- **Place & era:** grew up in the late-Soviet periphery; came of age as the USSR was ending — the generation that
  built Russia's post-1991 private tech sector from nothing, with no incumbent to inherit.
- **Schooling → university:** studied **applied mathematics at the Gubkin Russian State University of Oil and Gas**
  (O01, O02). A hard-quantitative, build-it formation — not finance, not law, not politics.
- **First ventures:** before Yandex, co-founded **CompTek International** and **InfiNet Wireless** (networking
  hardware) and served as CompTek CEO (O01). I.e., he was **already a builder-operator of real infrastructure
  companies** before the famous one — the pattern predates any investor audience.
- → **Likely formed:** engineering-first problem solving; comfort building category-defining infrastructure from
  zero; quantitative discipline; outsider's drive. **Frugality is NOT the signature** (unlike a Duan-style operator)
  — the signature is *technical ambition at national/global scale*.

*Adult track (compressed):* **Co-founded Yandex (1997)**, built it into Russia's dominant search + a full internet
ecosystem (maps, ride-hail, cloud, self-driving), **NASDAQ IPO 2011** (one of the year's largest tech IPOs), CEO
2000–2022. Crucible: when Russia invaded Ukraine, his position became untenable → **resigned as Yandex CEO mid-2022
and transferred his voting power to the board** (O07); **EU-sanctioned June 2023**; **publicly called the invasion
"barbaric" (Aug 2023)** — one of very few Russian-elite figures to do so; **EU sanctions lifted 12 Mar 2024** (O08);
engineered the **separation of the non-Russian assets** (AI cloud, Toloka, Avride, ClickHouse stake) from the
Russian operations (sold ~$5.4B, S017), **relisted on Nasdaq as NBIS Oct 2024**, and **returned as CEO (2024)** to
rebuild around AI infrastructure. Adult conduct **matches** the formation (builder re-builds; technical ambition
intact); the motivation/fire at 60+ is conspicuous — this is a chosen second act, not a coast.

*Dark-arc (required):*
- **Geopolitical entanglement.** He built and ran Russia's most important tech company for two decades under an
  increasingly authoritarian state; Yandex faced persistent questions over state pressure, content control, and a
  golden-share / government-influence arrangement. The sanctions were lifted, but the **history is a permanent
  reputational and geopolitical tail** — and the very fact (per the operator template) that public-image items like
  an origin story can be *curated* ("erasing Russia from the bio") is, in his case, **literal**, and is the tell to
  discount narrative and trust the unchooseable facts.
- **Control concentration.** He returned and re-took **voting control via a dual-class trust** — minority holders
  are along for the ride.
- **He left a lot of value on the table in Russia under duress** — a forced, discounted divestiture — which is a
  reminder that *exogenous* (political) risk, not just execution, can impair this kind of operator.

*Reliability axis:* documented unchooseable facts (Guryev birth, geologist father / music-teacher mother, Gubkin
applied-math degree, CompTek/InfiNet, 1997 Yandex, 2011 IPO) > dated third-party records of the 2022–24 sanctions
arc > **the company's own forward "outstanding 2026" framing** (discount the last). Trust the first; the third is
management narrative.

*Guardrails:* no determinism — the formation explains *why a builder rebuilds*, it does not guarantee the gigawatt
build succeeds; the **direction of his departure from formation** (from Russian-state-embedded champion → Western-
listed, sanctions-cleared AI-infra founder) is itself the signal that he re-pointed rather than retired.

**Core team (Korolenko / Chernin / Shtan):** the unchooseable, load-bearing fact is **tenure together**: these are
the people who **built Yandex's search, cloud, and AI infrastructure over 10–20 years**, then **left Russia together**
to do it again (O03, O04). Korolenko (~20 yrs infra, supercomputers) and Shtan run the technical build; Chernin
(~12 yrs Yandex Search/Maps) runs the commercial side. A team that has shipped large-scale infra together for two
decades AND chose to relocate and rebuild together is a **high-cohesion, high-complementarity** signal that no
freshly-assembled neocloud team can replicate.

### Team dark-arc / fracture inversion

- Where could the team break? (1) **Volozh key-man / exit / health / fresh geopolitical action** removing the
  controller; (2) **CFO friction** — the one outsider, holding the hardest job (financing the build), could clash
  or churn; (3) **talent poaching** of the thin ex-Yandex technical core by deeper-pocketed hyperscalers; (4)
  culture dilution as headcount scales from a tight relocated nucleus to thousands. → kill criteria: founder
  departure without a credible successor; CFO exit inside ~18 months; loss of ≥1 of Korolenko/Shtan.

### Verdict → durability ceiling

- **Team life-arc grade: 4 / 5.** A genuinely proven, cohesive, complementary, long-arc engineering team that has
  built this exact category before and held together through extreme adversity — that is rare and **raises** the
  durability ceiling versus a typical neocloud. It is held **below 5** by: (a) the **governance/control discount**
  (dual-class founder control, minority holders unprotected, Nasdaq "Controlled Company"); (b) **key-man
  concentration** on Volozh + a thin technical bench; (c) the **unproven capital-allocation seat** at the scale this
  build requires; (d) the **residual geopolitical tail**.
- **Ceiling effect:** the team quality argues this is *not* a flimsy story stock and **supports** carrying it as a
  durable franchise candidate rather than a pure cyclical trade — BUT the control + key-man + financing-unproven
  factors mean the operator grade **caps the high end**: it should not be underwritten to the very top
  durability tier on team strength alone while financing and gigawatt execution remain unproven.
- **Feeds Duan IC lens ("管理层是否靠谱"):** **Yes on competence and alignment-of-effort** (founder re-engaged, skin
  in the game, candid about GAAP); **qualified on minority alignment** (dual-class control) and **on the one skill
  the franchise now most needs** (large-scale capital allocation), which is the newest and least-tested.
