## 3. Verdict

The lean 6-module pipeline **correctly caught the Meta cost-panic washout.** On a still-dominant,
profitable, net-cash ad franchise trading at ~10x trailing near a multi-year low — with a dated,
just-fired cost-discipline catalyst and an industry-wide (not Meta-specific) ad downturn — the
framework produced a **STARTER with a real ~3.5% entry, an explicit add-on-confirmation path, and a
~9% durability-capped ceiling**, treating the Reality-Labs + founder-control overhang as a
**size cap, not a veto** (M5 −1, no hard veto; M1=0 → haircut not WATCH). META then returned
**+465% to 2026-06-16 vs SPY +102.6% / QQQ +170.2%** (beat SPY by ~363 pts), **beat SPY by ~192
pts in the first 12 months alone**, and drew down only **−2.9%** — the entry was within ~3% of the
absolute bottom. Axis-1 **PASS**, reasoning **sound**, dominant modules **M2/M3 (match)**, **no
veto (correctly)**, **no primary failure tag**.

The single deduction is METHODOLOGY §7's "too small loses points," and it lands on the **ceiling,
not the entry**: a ~9% cap held a `quality_compounder`-economics franchise (operator grade 3,
long-tenured team, net cash, growing core profit pool) to the **Confirmed tier and never Core**,
and the constraint that set that cap — the founder-control/Reality-Labs governance discount —
proved **value-positive** over the window (founder control *delivered* the cost turn and the
re-rate). This is the **same structural finding as the GOOGL case** (§5b/§5c addendum): a
founder/control-governance discount applied as a **tier demotion** rather than a **within-Core-band
haircut**, on a same-vintage founder-controlled franchise. With META now the **second** scored case
exhibiting it (GOOGL size-distance 1, META size-distance 1, both founder-control franchises, both
ceiling-capped one tier too low), the `PATCH_LEDGER.md → P1` hypothesis — apply the governance
discount **within** the Core band, operator-grade-conditioned, counted once, rather than as a
Confirmed-tier demotion — now has the **two corroborating winners (GOOGL + META)** that
`METHODOLOGY.md §2`'s staged note and `PROTOCOL.md §9` asked for before validation. (DIS, the
intended *low*-operator-grade counter-test, remains outstanding to confirm the discount is
operator-grade-conditioned rather than flat.) Net grade: a strong, well-reasoned PASS with a
minor, now-corroborated ceiling-calibration lean.
