# Researcher Background Profiles

Purpose: separate publicly verifiable background, self-description, and method
signals for outside research voices we may learn from.

## Evidence Rule

Do not treat a public persona's claimed resume as proof of investment skill.

Use three layers:

1. Verified / institutional: company site, official filings, employer pages,
   named publications, regulatory records.
2. Self-reported / media repeated: profile bio, interviews, channel pages,
   social posts.
3. Method-inferred: what the work product suggests about the person's training.

Only layer 1 can be treated as hard background evidence. Layers 2 and 3 can
explain why the work looks professional, but not prove the resume.

## 美股投资网 / TradesMax

Public claim:

- 美股投资网 says it was founded in Silicon Valley and focuses on U.S. stock
  research.
- Its official site says founder Ken was a former NYSE analyst.
- Its site says the core team includes Morgan Stanley analysts and Google/Meta
  engineers.
- A 2017 profile on its own site names the founder as Ken Zhang and describes
  prior roles as NYSE data analyst/data scientist, investment analyst, and
  financial engineer.

Reliability:

- Strong that this is their own stated positioning.
- Still needs independent verification before treating each employment detail as
  proven.

What their work product suggests:

- They likely come from a hybrid of financial-data product, sell-side research
  summarization, trading education, and content marketing.
- Their process emphasizes catalysts, options/dark-pool/flow data, analyst
  reports, earnings metrics, AI/data products, and retail-facing narrative.
- This is closer to a fintech/research-content shop than a pure buy-side fund.

Useful lessons:

- Topic engine.
- Catalyst calendar.
- Theme-to-value-chain mapping.
- Fast translation of institutional research into readable stock narratives.

Main caution:

- Their content can be promotional. We should use it as a scout, not as final
  evidence.

## Serenity / 白毛股神

Public claim:

- Chinese financial media describe Serenity as an anonymous overseas investor
  active on X.
- Media reports say he is not a traditional institutional investor.
- Reports repeat Serenity's self-description as an AI research scientist and
  RISC-V Foundation member.
- Some secondary articles claim a stronger technical background, including
  patents or academic publications, but those details require independent
  verification.
- X/Reddit traces point to the handle `@aleabitoreddit` / `AleaBito`.

Reliability:

- Strong that he is an online/X-native anonymous investor known for AI supply
  chain research.
- Medium that he has meaningful technical background, because the method and
  public bio both point there.
- Weak until independently verified for specific resume claims such as named
  employers, publications, patents, or NVIDIA offer claims.

What his work product suggests:

- More engineering/supply-chain than finance credential.
- Strong at physical bottlenecks: photonics, CPO, InP substrates, ASIC supply
  chains, power, robotics components.
- Looks like bottom-up technical due diligence plus high-risk small-cap trading.
- Not a conventional Buffett-style investor and not a normal sell-side analyst.

Useful lessons:

- Find what large AI winners must depend on.
- Search for scarce physical bottlenecks rather than obvious leaders.
- Reverse-engineer supply chains from BOM, patents, customer relationships,
  production capacity, and technical constraints.
- Ask "what breaks the whole system if it is missing?"

Main caution:

- Anonymous identity.
- Unaudited returns.
- Micro/small-cap reflexivity: public posts can move illiquid stocks.
- High volatility and concentrated risk are part of the style.

## Practical Takeaway

These two sources are professional-looking for different reasons:

- 美股投资网: institutional-style content production plus financial data tools.
- Serenity: technical-domain depth plus chokepoint hunting.

For our research OS, the right synthesis is:

- Use 美股投资网 for topic discovery and catalyst framing.
- Use Serenity for bottleneck and supply-chain imagination.
- Use Buffett/Munger/Duan for business quality, management, margin of safety,
  and temperament.
- Use our source policy and claim ledger to prevent being captured by charisma,
  marketing, or social reflexivity.
