# personal/ — 人生研究与投资

This folder is **separate** from the rest of `financial-analysis-lab/`. The
rest of the workspace (`companies/`, `sectors/`, `macro/`, `theses/`) is for
**market** investment research. This folder is for **life and career**
research and decisions — where to live, what to do, where to put time,
which path to bet on.

Same rigor (theses, evidence, decision logs), different subject.

## Layout

- `career-thesis/` — current career/path thesis and its evolution
  - `2026-05-thesis.md` — current working thesis (elevator framework +
    Chinese medical device出海 + AI imaging in international business)
- `research/` — supporting research that informs the career thesis
  - `_pending.md` — research items planned but not yet completed

## Rules

- Don't mix files from here into `companies/` / `sectors/` / `theses/`.
- When a career-thesis decision implies a market bet (e.g., long 联影/迈瑞),
  keep the *career* reasoning here and add a separate market memo in the
  appropriate `companies/` / `sectors/` folder, cross-linking the two.
- Update the thesis doc when the framing shifts. Older versions stay in
  `career-thesis/` with their date — don't delete history.
