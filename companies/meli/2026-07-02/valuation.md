**The asymmetry is favorable but not free.** The base case clears a 15%+ hurdle AT TODAY'S PRICE; even the plain bear (margins NEVER recover from trough, growth halves) is roughly breakeven because 40%+ revenue growth compounds absolute earnings. The only scenario with a material loss is the SEVERE BEAR — a genuine LatAm credit-cycle blowup (net margin to 5%) + growth collapse + deep de-rate. That is the real risk being underwritten, and it is a real (if lower-probability) tail.

- **Key swing factors:** (1) normalized net margin (does the 6.9% trough recover toward 10%+? — the IFRS-9/investment thesis); (2) revenue CAGR durability (competition from Shopee/Shein); (3) exit multiple (a growth de-rate to 20-25x is assumed even in the good cases — conservative). The single biggest downside driver is a CREDIT-BOOK deterioration that makes the margin compression permanent/worse, not a multiple problem.

## 3. Buy-below / entry discipline

Because at $1,763 the base case ALREADY clears ~15-24%/yr, the discipline here is NOT "wait for a much lower price" (as with HWM) — it is "size for the severe-bear tail." Reference levels:

| Trigger | Price | Basis |
|---|---:|---|
| STARTER (already actionable) | **<= ~$1,900** | Base 5y IRR clears ~15%+; bear ~breakeven. Today's $1,763 qualifies. Buy-below set slightly above spot to allow entry now with a small buffer. |
| ADD / higher-conviction | **<= ~$1,600** | Near the recent trough / +7% off 52wk low; base IRR pushes toward ~28%+; margin of safety on the severe-bear widens materially |
| Fat-pitch (back up the truck within the cyclical cap) | **<= ~$1,300-1,400** | A re-test below the 52wk low on a macro/credit scare with thesis intact -> severe-bear becomes ~breakeven, base ~30%+ |
| Do-not-chase / trim | **>= ~$2,500-2,700** | Back near the ATH; base IRR compresses below ~12% and you're paying up for the margin recovery before it's proven |

## 4. Verdict

**There IS a margin of safety here — in the growth, not the current multiple.** New money = **STARTER** (not WATCH): the base-case 5y IRR clears the hurdle at today's price and the bear is ~breakeven; only a genuine credit-cycle severe-bear loses materially. This is the OPPOSITE of the HWM/GEV "wonderful business, unwonderful price" pattern — here a wonderful-ish business has DE-RATED into a range where the math works, and the binding constraint is CONVICTION/completeness on the margin-and-credit inflection (unproven, needs 2-3 quarters), NOT price. Size for the tail: a cyclical-credit-exposed grower caps around the Confirmed tier (~8-12%), initiated as a Starter (~3-5%) until the margin inflection and credit-through-cycle are confirmed.
