## INTERPRETATION

- MELI is a genuine LatAm super-app flywheel (marketplace + Mercado Pago + Mercado Envíos + Mercado Crédito) in hyperscale (+49% Q1 revenue) that is **deliberately trading current margin for share and future scale** (free shipping, 1P, credit cards, cross-border). The drawdown (-31%) is the market's discomfort with (a) margin compression and (b) an 87%-growing credit book that doubled provisions. Business quality is high; the debate is whether the margin sacrifice is high-ROIC investment (bull) or a structural/competitive/credit problem (bear).
- DIVERSIFICATION: MELI is a LatAm consumer + fintech + logistics factor, essentially UNCORRELATED to the user's BTC/GOOGL/NBIS "US AI-capex + liquidity" single factor. Its risk driver is LatAm macro/FX (Brazil rates, Argentine peso), not US AI capex. Strong diversification value — the primary reason to own it.
