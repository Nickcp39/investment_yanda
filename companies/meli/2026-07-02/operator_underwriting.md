## Founder & Core Team Life-Arc — Marcos Galperin

### Formation (unchooseable -> value formation; highest weight)

- **Family & home:** Born 1971-10-31, Buenos Aires; fourth of five sons of a **business-wealth family** — the Galperins own **SADESA, a large leather manufacturer**. **-> He was born into affluence, not hardship.** This is the single most important honesty flag against the investor's "hardship-formed technical builder" archetype: his socioeconomic start was privileged. (SADESA's "one of the world's largest" superlative is secondary; the fact of a substantial family industrial business is well-documented.)
- **Place & era — the genuinely unchooseable macro:** he came of age through Argentina's **late-1980s hyperinflation** (thousands of % in 1989-90) and later the **2001 sovereign default / corralito**. An Argentine entrepreneur's worldview forged by serial monetary collapse — this IS his truest unchooseable formative signal, and it shows up in a lifelong obsession with capital efficiency, dollarized thinking, and distrust of "changing rules." (Inferred from age+geography+consistent later commentary; not a first-person autobiographical quote.)
- **Schooling / university:** St. Andrew's Scots School (elite bilingual private school, greater Buenos Aires) -> **Wharton (UPenn), BS Finance 1994 (honors)** -> **Stanford GSB MBA 1999.** **-> Formation is finance-first, not engineering.** He is a finance-trained builder, NOT a hardship-formed technical founder.
- **First career:** **YPF** (Argentina's oil major) Capital Markets / Futures & Options 1994-97; **JP Morgan** NY Fixed Income (summer 1998). Finance-markets roots.
- **-> Likely formed:** capital efficiency, survival instinct, contrarian conviction, comfort operating amid macro chaos. Consistent with his actual conduct (surviving the crash, 25-yr efficient compounding, one-share-one-vote).

### Adult track (compressed)

- **Founding (1999):** conceived MELI at Stanford (inspired by an eBay case), co-founded Aug 1999 with **Hernán Kazah** and **Stelleo Tolda**. Raised ~$7.6M seed (Hicks Muse/JPM Partners/Goldman) and ~$46.5M (May 2000) closing INTO the NASDAQ crash.
- **Crucible behavior:** during the dot-com collapse cut headcount 200->100 and marketing ~90%, consolidated to Buenos Aires, reached profitability ~2005 with ~1/4 of the war chest intact while 80+ regional rivals died. **Neutralized eBay** (took a 19.5% stake 2001, agreed to stay out of LatAm; later exited). Built **Mercado Pago (2003)**, Envíos, and Crédito ground-up. Fended off Amazon.
- **The adult conduct MATCHES the formation:** the survival-under-fire crucible (1999-2005) plus 25 years of capital-efficient, contrarian, reinvestment-over-harvest building. The current "invest, don't harvest" margin sacrifice is the SAME playbook that built Brazil credit over five years.

### Dark-arc (required)

- **Born-into-wealth + finance pedigree:** he is NOT the scrappy, hardship-formed technical builder the investor most prizes. He is a privileged, finance-trained owner-operator. That does NOT make him a courtier — but it is the honest deduction on the archetype axis.
- **The reinvestment bet as hubris risk:** "now is precisely the right moment to invest boldly" — a 25-yr winner can still over-invest at the top of a credit cycle. The 87% credit-book growth + margin sacrifice is either disciplined moat-defense (his track record says trust it) or a value-destructive stretch (the bear). His formation biases him toward aggressive-but-efficient growth; the failure mode is under-appreciating a LatAm credit cycle he has mostly seen only from the deposit side.
- **Political entanglement:** relocated to **Uruguay** (2002+; high-profile Argentina departure Dec 2019 after the Peronist win — "would not return regardless of who wins"); now among the business voices closest to **President Milei**. Report neutral: this is a pro-market operator voting with his feet against populist policy, consistent with his formation — NOT a political courtier seeking state favor. (It DOES create Argentine political-optics risk.)

### Reliability axis

Documented facts (Wharton/Stanford, YPF/JPM, 1999 founding, dot-com survival, eBay stake, one-share-one-vote, ~7% Trust, 2026 succession) > the "contrarian visionary" hagiography. The track record is verifiable and strong.
