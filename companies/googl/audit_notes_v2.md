# GOOGL Audit Notes v2

Last updated: 2026-06-16

Purpose: prevent premature completion language during the full Google rerun.

## Audit Position

GOOGL v2 can be summarized as a strong business under active re-underwriting, not a finished investment decision.

The evidence so far supports the following:

- Search revenue has not collapsed during the AI transition.
- Search unit economics still show paid-click and CPC growth.
- Cloud growth and operating income improved sharply in Q1 2026.
- Capex, debt, and financing needs increased enough to make owner earnings the central question.

The evidence does not yet support the following:

- AI Search monetization is equal to or better than traditional Search.
- 2026-2027 AI capex is mostly high-return growth capex.
- Cloud backlog will convert at today-like margins.
- The public stock price offers a margin of safety.
- Berkshire or H&H ownership alone proves the stock is buyable.
