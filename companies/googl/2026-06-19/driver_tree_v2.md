# GOOGL Driver Tree v2

Last updated: 2026-06-16

Purpose: translate the v2 thesis into observable drivers. This is the operating map for the next research pass.

Core thesis:

> Alphabet is attractive only if it keeps control of commercial intent and converts AI infrastructure capex into higher owner earnings per share.

---

## 1. Revenue Driver Tree

| Driver | Current Read | Growth Case | Decline Case | Evidence Needed | Monitor / Threshold |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Search & other revenue | Q1 2026 +19% YoY; paid clicks +13%; CPC +5% | AI expands query volume and creates higher-intent ad formats | AI answers reduce ad load, click attribution, and RPM | AI Overviews / AI Mode RPM vs traditional Search; commercial query mix | downgrade if Search & other falls to high single digits for 2 quarters without macro explanation |
| Search price / CPC | CPC +5% | Advertiser ROI remains strong; auction liquidity holds | Agent / retail media / vertical search takes high-intent budget | CPC by vertical, conversion rate, TAC, advertiser ROI commentary | monitor CPC trend and TAC mix |
| Search volume / paid clicks | paid clicks +13% | AI increases usage and query frequency | zero-click answers reduce paid-click opportunities | paid click disclosure, query mix, AI penetration | monitor paid clicks vs AI feature adoption |
| YouTube ads | Q1 2026 +11% YoY | Shorts monetization improves; connected TV grows; AI tools boost creator supply | TikTok / Reels / AI video compress time spent and CPM | watch time, Shorts monetization, ad load, subscription mix | downgrade if ads slow while engagement also weakens |
| Google Network | Q1 2026 declined | open web weakness contained; owned surfaces offset | open web ad network structurally decays | Network revenue, privacy changes, publisher take rate | treat continued decline as signal of value-chain shift |
| Cloud revenue | Q1 2026 +63% YoY; backlog >$460B | AI workloads and enterprise data gravity convert backlog into high-margin revenue | price war and low-margin compute make revenue growth less valuable | RPO conversion, backlog duration, customer concentration, AI workload mix | monitor growth plus margin together, not growth alone |
| Subscriptions / platforms / devices | Q1 2026 +19% YoY; 350M paid subscriptions in FWP | Gemini plans and Google One become consumer AI monetization layer | consumer AI willingness to pay weakens; hardware remains low margin | paid subs, ARPU, churn, Gemini paid plan adoption | upgrade if paid AI plans add recurring high-margin revenue |

## 2. Expense / Capex Driver Tree

| Driver | Current Read | Improvement Case | Deterioration Case | Evidence Needed | Monitor / Threshold |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| TAC | TAC rate down to 19.7% from 20.6% | Mix shifts to owned Search; distribution costs stable | default deals or regulatory changes raise TAC | TAC by segment, default agreement status | watch TAC rate after DOJ outcomes |
| Cloud gross / operating margin | Q1 2026 Cloud operating margin about 32.9% | utilization and TPU cost advantage sustain margin | AI compute price war or utilization reversal | multi-quarter Cloud margin, utilization commentary | downgrade if margin falls >5pp for 2 quarters |
| AI capex | Q1 2026 capex $35.7B; 2026 guide $180-190B; 2027 significantly higher | capex is high-return growth investment | capex is defensive spending to protect old moat | capex split, depreciation life, unit compute cost, ROI framework | downgrade if capex/OCF >70% for full year without ROI evidence |
| Depreciation | Q1 2026 P&E depreciation $6.5B vs $4.5B prior year | revenue growth absorbs depreciation | depreciation catches up and compresses margins | asset lives, in-service timing, server/network depreciation | monitor D&A growth vs revenue growth |
| SBC / dilution | FY2025 SBC large; Q1 2026 buyback paused | buybacks resume after capex peak; dilution controlled | ATM / preferred / stock issuance and paused buybacks lift share count | share count, SBC, net buybacks, ATM issuance | downgrade if share count rises and FCF/share stagnates |
| Debt / financing cost | debt and equity financing rising | balance sheet remains flexible; funding supports high-ROIC growth | cost of capital rises while returns lag | debt balance, interest expense, preferred dilution | monitor financing as a permanent funding source, not one-off |

## 3. Owner Earnings Branches

| Branch | Description | What Must Be True | Result |
|---|---|---|---|
| Growth Capex | AI capex builds scarce infrastructure and new high-return revenue streams | Search AI RPM holds; Cloud margin stays high; capex peaks; FCF/share recovers | quality compounder remains intact |
| Defensive Capex | AI capex is required to protect Search and Cloud relevance | Search slows; capex stays high; depreciation and dilution rise | owner earnings multiple should compress |
| Mixed / Delay | AI capex eventually works, but payback takes longer than market expects | revenue grows but FCF/share lags for several years | valuation must be materially lower to compensate |
