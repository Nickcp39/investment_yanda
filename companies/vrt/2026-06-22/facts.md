## Business position

Only broad-line single-vendor "grey-space" integrator shipping a **co-engineered power + thermal stack** for an NVIDIA rack. Peers split the chain: Schneider (breadth peer), ABB/Eaton (power-weighted), SMCI (server box, partial threat), Boyd/CoolIT/Modine (cold-plate/CDU — sit *inside* VRT's BoM). Moat = owning the **integrated reference design + speed-to-deploy**, not any one commodity component. The **ThermoKey close (06-12)** extends the thermal chain into heat-rejection and shores up the weak EMEA region.
