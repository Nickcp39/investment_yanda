FOR: rising rack density is mechanical, not optional (GB200 NVL72 ~132kW, GB300 ~142kW; above ~50kW air cooling fails) → **liquid becomes mandatory and VRT's $-content per rack steps up**; VRT **co-authors NVIDIA reference designs** (closest thing to a standard) and is extending to **800V DC**; #1 liquid-cooling share (~11.3%); $15B backlog → visibility into 2027.
