1. **D — hyperscaler capex slope rolls** (the shared switch). VRT is the **most exposed** name in the basket; any 2027 digestion hits it **first and hardest**; **no floor.** Arithmetic of beta, not narrative.

The two risks **compound**: highest beta + no floor + **lost demand gauge**. *Don't let "margins beat" distract from "we can no longer see orders."*
3. **B — system-layer commoditization / Schneider.** Slower-moving but structural; 800V DC is the defense.
