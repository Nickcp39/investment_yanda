## 1. The valuation problem in one line
At **$2,288.92** SanDisk trades at ~76x *trailing* and ~17–19x *peak-annualized-forward* earnings — and **above the average sell-side 12-month target (~$1,751–1,843)** and above third-party DCF fair value [S015/S020]. The only way to justify the price is to capitalize **peak NAND earnings as if they were permanent.** That is the textbook cyclical valuation trap (a low forward multiple at the top of the cycle).

## 2. Why trailing/forward P/E is misleading here
- NAND is a commodity. GM expanded **22.5% → 78.4% in four quarters** [S001/S017]. A forward P/E of ~17x looks cheap only because the **E** is peak-cycle earnings power.
- The correct lens for a cyclical is **normalized mid-cycle earnings × a cycle-aware multiple**, plus an explicit haircut for the fact that the entire AI-memory complex is at a shortage apex with capacity (BiCS10, Kioxia +40–66% capex, 3rd Kitakami fab) arriving in 2027 [S009/S010].

## 4. Margin of safety
- **MOS for NEW money: deeply negative.** Probability-weighted fair value ~$470 vs price $2,288.92 → the market price embeds ~5x the central fair value. There is no margin of safety at any reading of the distribution.
- **Downside if the cycle reverts:** −80% to −95% (toward $100–450) is on the table for a commodity peaking at a parabolic valuation — the asymmetry is inverted (large downside, little upside) for a new buyer.
- **Buy-below (new money):** I will not set a "buy-below" near the price. A *defensible* starter zone would require the stock to come back toward the **bull/base boundary (~$450–700)** AND a confirmed-higher normalized margin floor — i.e. a >70% drawdown from here plus durability proof. Practically: **no new money; buy-below ≈ $600** as an anchor only (still requires durability confirmation).

## 5. What WOULD justify a higher fair value
- Primary-confirmed NBM economics showing a durable margin floor materially above prior cycles (e.g. GM staying >45% through a downcycle), AND
- Evidence the AI-storage demand curve outgrows the 2027 capacity additions for multiple years.
Until then, the durable-reset case is a *hope*, and the methodology caps a cyclical below Core regardless.
