## 4. Balance sheet & capital return
- Near net-cash: cash $293.8M + mkt sec $28.2M (Dec'25); ~$400M cash+investments at Q1'26; short-term debt $0 (small revolver draw ~$165M for April M&A). Equity ~$3.14B. **Survives any semi downturn** → no bankruptcy risk, downside is de-rate not permanent impairment.

## 5. Quality verdict
**Good, cyclical.** High-margin duopoly cash machine with a fortress balance sheet, but earnings are cycle-distorted (2026 peak) and robotics drains cash. **Not exceptional** — the durability ceiling for sizing is capped **below Core** (cyclical), and today's price sits on peak earnings.
