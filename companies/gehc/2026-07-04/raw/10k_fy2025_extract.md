## Business Description (as filed)
GE HealthCare Technologies Inc. — global healthcare solutions provider, advanced medical technology + pharmaceutical diagnostics + AI/cloud/software tools for clinicians. Operates in >160 countries. ~54,000 employees, ~11,100 engineers/scientists.

## Segments (legacy 4-segment structure, as filed for FY2025)
1. **Imaging**: Molecular Imaging, CT, Magnetic Resonance, Women's Health, X-ray — "AI-enabled software and applications that help clinicians improve productivity, address staff shortages, and deliver better patient outcomes."
2. **Advanced Visualization Solutions (AVS)**: Specialized Ultrasound and Procedural Guidance — comprehensive care ultrasound, women's health ultrasound, cardiovascular interventional solutions, surgical innovations.
3. **Patient Care Solutions (PCS)**: Monitoring Solutions and Life Support Solutions — patient monitoring, diagnostic cardiology, maternal-infant care, anesthesia, consumables, digital solutions.
4. **Pharmaceutical Diagnostics (PDx)**: Contrast and radiopharmaceutical imaging agents for global radiology/nuclear medicine.
