### Per-person life-arc — formation first (the unchooseable years are the most truthful)

> Core insight: an adult's public image, statements, even résumé can be **chosen and performed**
> (Volozh "erasing Russia from his bio" is the tell). But **family, where they grew up,
> primary / middle / high school, university, first job** are mostly **unchooseable** and happened
> **before** there was any reason to perform for investors — so they are the truest signal of how values
> were *formed*. **Weight this most.**

**<Name>**

*Formation (unchooseable → value formation; highest weight):*
- Family & home (parents' work/values, wealth/hardship, intact/broken, minority/immigrant, religion/culture):
- Place & era grew up in (country/city/rural, political-economic backdrop, generational baseline):
- Schooling primary → middle → high (elite/struggling; what it instilled; key relationships formed here):
- University & education (field, institution, why; mentors):
- First job / first venture (how they actually behaved before any audience):
- → What this likely formed (cautious pattern, not determinism): frugality / risk / engineering-first / integrity code / resilience …

*Adult track (compressed):* prior ventures & outcomes; crucible behavior; **does adult conduct match the values formed above?**; motivation now + fire; long-term skin in the game.

*Dark-arc (required):* bear case — where they failed people / broke promises / red flags.

*Reliability axis (chooseable vs unchooseable):* documented unchooseable facts (birth, family, schools attended, era) > third-party early-career records > **the founder's own later origin story** (origin myths get polished — trust facts, discount narrative).

*Guardrails:* no determinism — formation is high-signal evidence of value *origins*, not fate; flag where the adult **departed** from their formation (the direction of departure is itself a signal). Evidence only; no unsupported psychological diagnosis.
