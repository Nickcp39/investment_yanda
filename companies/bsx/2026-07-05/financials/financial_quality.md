## Financial Quality Verdict

**STRONG multi-year trend, ONE early-warning quarter, ONE significant unidentified one-off.** BSX's 5-year revenue/margin trajectory (revenue nearly doubling, operating margin +800bps) is genuinely excellent and materially stronger than the GEHC exemplar's margin-compression pattern. Balance sheet leverage is currently moderate (1.87x, well within covenant) though set to step up materially once Penumbra closes (H2 2026, not yet reflected in current figures). The Q1 2026 deceleration (+11.6% vs FY2025's +19.9%) is the first sign of the guidance-cut narrative showing up in top-line growth, but is one data point, not yet a confirmed multi-quarter trend. The unidentified Q1 2026 GAAP/adjusted EPS divergence (O5) is a genuine, honestly-flagged gap that should be resolved before placing full confidence in the GAAP earnings trajectory — this dossier's valuation work uses the adjusted EPS basis specifically to avoid building on an unconfirmed one-off.
