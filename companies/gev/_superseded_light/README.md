## The one thing to remember

GEV owns a **real, currently-binding physical bottleneck** (3-player heavy-duty gas-turbine oligopoly, slots sold out through 2029, +10–20% pricing, prepaid reservation deposits) and a **sticky services annuity** ($87B services RPO on ~7,000 installed turbines). That is among the best moats in industrials. **But** at $1,109 (~46–51× clean forward EBITDA, premium to Siemens Energy) the market prices the toll-booth as *permanent*, while the **equipment** pricing premium is a cyclical peak hostage to the hyperscaler capex slope. The services rent is worth paying up for; do not pay a permanent-rent multiple for a cyclical equipment peak. **Excellent business, wrong price.**
