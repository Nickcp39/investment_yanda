- I3. **FY2026 clean EBITDA**: Midpoint guidance = $44.5–45.5B × 13% margin = **~$5.7–6.0B adj EBITDA**. This is the relevant current-year multiple basis.
- I4. **(derived) Clean forward EV/EBITDA**: EV ~$292B ÷ $5.7–6.0B ≈ **46–51x** — identical to light-run estimate. CONFIRMED.
- I5. **(derived) Market cap ÷ FY2026 FCF guide midpoint $7.0B** = **~43x P/FCF**. Premium multiple pricing durable rent + 2028 targets.
- I6. **Wind losses**: $382M Q1 alone; guide for ~$400M full-year losses implies back-end improvement needed. Tariff headwind $250–350M company-wide ongoing.
- I7. **Prolec remeasurement**: Non-recurring $3.99B gain from fair-value step-up of previously held 50% stake. Will not recur.
- I8. **2028 EBITDA if targets met**: $52B × 20% = ~$10.4B. At current EV ~$292B → **16x 2028 EV/EBITDA**. The market is paying ~40–50x for a business priced to deliver ~16x at a 2-year horizon if execution is perfect.
- I9. **Services RPO durability**: $87B+ services backlog is multi-year contracted revenue (gas turbine maintenance agreements, nuclear services). Much harder to cancel than equipment orders. This is the "annuity" component.
- I10. **Net cash fortress**: Net cash ~$7B at Q1 2026; cumulative $22B+ FCF target 2025-2028 (=~$5.5B/yr avg) vs current $298B mkt cap (2.2% annual FCF yield on stated guidance, not yet growing to targets).
