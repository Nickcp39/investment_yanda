### Net

Cleaner owner-operator profile than NBIS (no super-voting control, no conglomerate sprawl); the trade-off is a much bigger balance-sheet bet in-flight.

### The 3 that matter most (ranked)
1. **(c) Calpine execution** — the only genuinely live, CEG-specific risk: deleverage 3.8x→2.0x stalls, or a divestiture slips, or accretion under-delivers. Arithmetic, not narrative.
2. **The "capped flagship + no new PPA" trap** (a cross of (b) and the PPA structure) — Crane's upside is sold to MSFT; if no *new* AI PPA signs and power softens toward the floor, the equity is dead money at ~24x even with the moat intact.
3. **(a)-residual** — a hostile final PJM co-location *definition* (C016). Low probability after the 5-0 order, and CEG's front-of-meter flagship is insulated, but the definition is genuinely still open.

- Calpine deleverage hits ~2.0x on/ahead of schedule with the LS Power cash in → kill (c) defused.
