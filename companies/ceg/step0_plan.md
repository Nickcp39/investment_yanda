## Research Question

```text
1. (LEAD — bottleneck) Is CEG's existing nuclear fleet a DURABLE scarcity
   rent — carbon-free baseload you physically cannot build new, sold to
   datacenters at premium PPAs with a PTC price floor — or is the AI-power
   premium already fully priced and exposed to a single regulatory switch
   (FERC behind-the-meter co-location)?
2. (DECISION) At today's price, BUY / NO-BUY / WATCH, and at what
   price/evidence each becomes right.
3. (KILL) What breaks the scarcity-rent thesis?
```

## Thesis hypothesis (analyst prior — knowledge to 2026-01, MUST verify in Block 1)

- Largest US nuclear operator (~21-22 GW), spun from Exelon **2022**.
- **Three Mile Island Unit 1 restart** (rebranded Crane Clean Energy Center) under
  a 20-yr PPA with **Microsoft**, announced **Sept 2024** — the iconic "AI reopens a
  shut nuclear plant" deal.
- Agreed to acquire **Calpine** (gas + geothermal), announced **Jan 2025** — shifts
  the carbon-free-purity story; verify leverage / accretion / close status.
- Floor protection: IRA nuclear **production tax credit (PTC)** ≈ caps downside on
  power price; upside = premium datacenter PPAs above merchant.
- Key risk = **FERC**: the Nov 2024 rejection of the Talen/Amazon behind-the-meter
  co-location amendment put the whole "plug datacenter straight into the plant"
  model in regulatory limbo. This is the swing variable, not the fuel.

## Why now

- Cleanest "AI shortage of power → nuclear cash flow" expression; the generation-side
  counterweight to GEV (equipment) and VRT (downstream) in this batch.
- Fresh primary data: post-TMI-deal disclosures, Calpine deal docs, latest FERC posture.

## Non-Goals

- Not a power-price timing trade.
- Do not treat the Microsoft headline as the thesis — verify PPA terms / FERC path.
- No memo before evidence layer is built.

## Kill criteria (pre-registered)

- FERC durably blocks/curtails behind-the-meter co-location → premium-PPA model capped.
- Power prices fall toward / below PTC floor (upside, not just downside, compresses).
- Calpine deal proves dilutive / over-levered / re-carbonizes the story.
- Nuclear PTC repealed or weakened.
- **Shared switch:** hyperscaler capex guidance slope turns down (batch-common variable).
