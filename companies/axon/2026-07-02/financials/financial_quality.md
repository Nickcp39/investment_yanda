- **Revenue +34%/yr CAGR (2021-25)** — exceptional, durable top-line; **9 consecutive quarters of 30%+ growth**; gross margin stable ~60%.
- **The central quality fact:** GAAP operating margin oscillates around zero (and went to **-2.2% in FY2025**) NOT because the business is unprofitable, but because **stock-based compensation ($325M in 2024, $634M in 2025) overwhelms operating income.** The SBC is driven by the milestone-vesting CEO Performance Award + employee XSPP: as market cap crosses each $1B tranche, large non-cash expense is recognized. Adjusted EBITDA (25.5% margin, $710M) and non-GAAP EPS ($6.85) strip this out.

## Balance sheet

- Cash + short-term investments **$1.7B** (FY25) / $731M (Q1'26 seasonal draw); senior + convertible notes ~$1.8B principal; **net debt only ~$112M**. Well-capitalized; can self-fund growth, M&A, and the convertible. No balance-sheet risk.

## Owner-earnings sanity

- FY25 adj EBITDA $710M; FY25 FCF $155M -> only ~22% EBITDA->FCF conversion (weak, due to WC + cash taxes + hardware). FY26E FCF guide $450M would lift conversion toward ~55-60% of adj EBITDA — a key thing to verify.
- At ~$49B EV: EV/FY25 adj EBITDA ~70x; P/FY25 non-GAAP NI ~87x; P/FY26E FCF ~110x. Whatever lens you use, the price capitalizes the (real, high-quality) growth very richly.

## Verdict

Financial reality **confirms a genuine, fast-growing, wide-moat SaaS-plus-hardware franchise (signal +1, not +2).** The +1 rather than +2 reflects two honest deductions vs a clean compounder like HWM: (1) **GAAP earnings are masked by very large milestone SBC** — the economics are real but you must underwrite on adjusted figures AND price the dilution; (2) **FCF is thin and lumpy relative to the multiple** (TTM FCF $20M; conversion weak), so the "quality" is more growth-and-ARR than cash-gushing-today. The financials are strong and improving — but, as with HWM, **the financials are not the binding constraint; the price is (M6).**
