## The one-paragraph thesis

AXON is a public-safety near-monopoly and a genuine quality-growth compounder: TASER (near-monopoly less-lethal) + body cameras are the hardware wedge that locks agencies into the Evidence.com / Axon Cloud SaaS + AI (Draft One) + counter-drone (Dedrone) system-of-record — a sticky, ~60%-gross-margin, **125%-net-retention** recurring-revenue flywheel with **$14.3B of pre-sold bookings (~5x revenue)** and a long multi-vector runway (federal, international ~20%, AI +700%, counter-drone +300%). Nine straight quarters of 30%+ growth; FY2025 revenue $2.8B, adj EBITDA $710M (25.5%). The founder-CEO, **Rick Smith**, is a rare true builder for this book — he founded TASER in a Tucson garage in 1993 after two childhood friends were murdered, nearly went bankrupt on that conviction, pivoted weapons→cameras→cloud→AI ahead of the market, took **no salary** under a 10-year all-milestone option award, and runs clean **one-share-one-vote** governance. It even de-correlates from the book's AI-capex factor. Everything about the business and the operator says want it. The price — after a +46% 8-session melt-up — says wait.
