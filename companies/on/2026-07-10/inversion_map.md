1. **The recovery is already in the price (primary risk).** Stock is **+115% off its 52-week low ($44.56)** and trades ~33x forward / ~67.9x trailing on downcycle earnings. The auto/industrial inflection is *consensus* and *visible* — the cheap cyclical entry (which existed near $45–55) is **gone**. 5. **Cyclical double-dip.** Auto/industrial demand relapses (macro, EV pause) → back toward trough EPS and the low-$50s.
