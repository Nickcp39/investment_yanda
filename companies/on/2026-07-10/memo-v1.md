# Working Memo v1 — ON (onsemi) · as_of 2026-07-10

**DRAFT (DECISION_DRAFT).** New money **WATCH** · Existing **HOLD** · $95.96.

## The one-paragraph story
onsemi is a cyclical automotive/industrial **power + image-sensing** semiconductor company (IDM, owns SiC substrate) that is **inflecting out of a multi-quarter downcycle**: Q1'26 revenue $1,513M (+5% Y/Y), automotive $797M (+5% — the first up-quarter after seven straight declines), non-GAAP GM recovering to 38.5%, FCF $217M, net debt only −$603M. It is a decent, improving business with a disciplined turnaround CEO and a $6B buyback. **But it is not the thing this batch is hunting for.**

## Why it fails the batch thesis
The batch asks for a "demand-exploded + supply-choked + already-earning" robotics bottleneck like NVDA-in-compute. onsemi's **robotics revenue is ≈ 0%** — design-wins and Embedded World demos only ("inductive position sensing for humanoid robotics"), plus the just-announced **$7B all-stock Synaptics "Physical AI" acquisition** (closes mid-2027, ~13% dilution) which is a *narrative*, not revenue. Its real growth-cash is (1) the auto/industrial cyclical recovery and (2) a genuine but small **AI-datacenter POWER** ramp (~$250M'25 → ~$500M'26E — power for datacenters, not robots). The components onsemi would sell into robots are **commodity** (38.5% GM, 4+ rivals, Sony pressuring image sensors, SiC oversupply) — no CUDA-style bottleneck.

## Why WATCH, not STARTER, even at face-value quality
**No margin of safety.** The stock is **+115% off its $44.56 low** and **−28.9% off its $134.92 high**, at ~33x forward / ~23x mid-cycle non-GAAP earnings. Base fair value ≈ $68 (16x $4.25 mid-cycle EPS); even the **bull** fair value (~$90) is *below* the current $95.96. Asymmetry is negative (bull −6% / base −29% / bear −53%). The market has already priced the recovery *and* an unearned robotics option. Capital-cycle caution is **PEAK-lean on valuation**: visible, consensus recovery after a doubling is late-for-a-new-buyer, not a trough entry.

## What would change the call
- **Price:** pullback into **~$65–72** restores a margin of safety on mid-cycle earnings → structured STARTER 3–5% (cyclical → capped below Core).
- **Thesis:** a **dated, sustainable** robotics/AI-DC-power **revenue** line (not design-wins) + confirmed 2H'26 gross-margin step toward mid-40s → upgrades the growth case.
- **Kill:** GM stalls <40%, SiC/CIS share loss, auto relapse, or Synaptics dilution without accretion.

## Bottom line
A quality-improving cyclical wearing a robotics label. Own the recovery only with a margin of safety; do not pay 33x forward for a robotics option worth ≈$0 of cash. **WATCH.**
