## Four reportable segments (as filed)
1. **Established Pharmaceutical Products (EPD)** — branded generics, primarily ex-US/emerging markets
2. **Diagnostic Products** — Core Laboratory, Rapid/POC diagnostics, Molecular, newly-added Cancer Diagnostics (post-Exact Sciences)
3. **Nutritional Products** — pediatric and adult nutrition (Similac, Ensure, Glucerna, PediaSure)
4. **Medical Devices** — Diabetes Care (FreeStyle Libre), Cardiovascular/Structural Heart, Electrophysiology, Rhythm Management, Heart Failure, Neuromodulation

## Named risk factors (as characterized in press release commentary, not verbatim 10-K risk-factor text)
- China market conditions, incl. volume-based procurement (VBP) programs, cited as a specific headwind to Diagnostics segment revenue
- COVID-19 testing revenue runoff continuing to be a drag on Diagnostics organic growth comps
- Nutrition segment cited a specific contract loss plus lower volumes
