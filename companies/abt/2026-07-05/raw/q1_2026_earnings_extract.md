## Headline results
- Total net sales: $11,164M (+7.8% reported / +3.7% comparable — comparable basis excludes FX, the Exact Sciences acquisition, and a structural-heart distribution agreement change)
- Net income (GAAP): $1,077M (down 18.7% YoY)
- GAAP diluted EPS: $0.61 (down from $0.76 prior year)
- Adjusted diluted EPS: $1.15 (+5.5-6% YoY)
- Management-cited headwinds: higher financing costs from the Exact Sciences acquisition; weak respiratory season affecting rapid/POC diagnostics testing volume
