## Founder & Core Team Life-Arc — Christophe Fouquet

### Formation (unchooseable -> value formation; highest weight)

- **Place & era:** French national, born 1973; came up in the **Grenoble** science/engineering ecosystem (France's microelectronics + physics hub — CEA-Leti, STMicro heritage). Formation is **hard-science, applied-physics-first**, not finance or law.
- **Schooling:** **MSc in Physics, Institut Polytechnique de Grenoble (1997).** A builder's education — the tell of a technologist operator, the opposite of the political-courtier/MBA-financier archetype this investor distrusts.
- **First career:** Global Product Manager at **Applied Materials**, then Director of Marketing at **KLA-Tencor** — i.e. he learned the semiconductor *capital-equipment* industry from the inside (both the deposition/etch and the metrology sides) before ASML.
- **-> Likely formed:** deep respect for the physics and the roadmap; a product/technology instinct over an empire/deal instinct; patience for decade-long R&D bets (EUV took ~20 years and ~EUR billions before it paid off).

### Adult track (compressed)

- **Joined ASML 2008** in marketing/product management — an *earned*, internal 16-year rise, not a lateral parachute.
- **EVP EUV (2018):** took ownership of the single most important — and most doubted — program in the company. Under his watch **EUV went from "promising but unreliable" to the industrialized monopoly** that now prints every leading-edge chip. This is the crucible: he built the thing the whole thesis rests on.
- **Chief Business Officer (2022) -> President & CEO (2024-04-25).** A clean internal succession from Peter Wennink; continuity of strategy (R&D-led, High-NA, service annuity, disciplined capital return).

### Dark-arc (required)

- **Short CEO tenure:** ~2 years in the top seat. His *program* record (EUV) is elite and verifiable; his *CEO* record (whole-company allocation, crisis navigation, cycle management) is still early. The China/export-control storm and the AI-capex cycle are his first real CEO-level tests — passing so far (raised guide, defended the EUV-in-China allegation, kept margins up as China fell).
- **Buyback at a rich multiple:** continuing the EUR12B program (EUR1.1B in Q1) with the stock near all-time highs / ~62x is the one allocation flag — defensible for a monopoly with net cash, but not obviously value-additive at this price.
- **Transparency reduction:** dropping quarterly bookings disclosure in 2026 slightly lowers accountability at exactly the moment order visibility matters most (China cliff + AI cycle).

### Reliability axis

Documented facts (Grenoble physics degree, AMAT/KLA roles, the EUV industrialization, internal 16-yr rise, clean governance) >> any polished narrative. The record is verifiable and strongly positive.

## Verdict -> durability ceiling

- **Team life-arc grade: 4.5/5** — a hardship-formed technical builder who *personally built the monopoly*, running a world-class engineering institution with clean, investor-favorable governance. This is exactly the operator archetype this investor favors (contrast: the political-courtier/pure-finance operator he distrusts). Capped just below 5 only by the short CEO tenure, the buyback-at-highs flag, and the bookings-disclosure reduction.
- **Ceiling effect:** operator quality **supports a high durability ceiling** for the business — it does NOT argue for a haircut. The size ceiling here is set by (a) deep semi-cyclicality and (b) the AI-factor-concentration in this specific book — not by the operator, and certainly not by price-independent quality.
- Feeds Duan IC lens ("管理层是否靠谱"): **非常靠谱 (highly reliable)** on capability, integrity and domain fit; the only watch-items are the rich-multiple buyback and reduced disclosure.
