## The one-paragraph thesis

ASML is the sole producer of EUV lithography on earth — the single deepest chokepoint in the semiconductor stack. TSMC, Samsung and Intel cannot make a leading-edge chip without it, so it collects a monopoly toll on every advanced logic and DRAM wafer, upstream of NVDA GPUs, Google TPUs and AWS Trainium. High-NA EUV extends the moat a generation; a ~28%-of-sales service annuity compounds on the installed base; the balance sheet is net cash; and the CEO (Christophe Fouquet) is the physicist who personally industrialized EUV — exactly the hardship-formed technical builder this book favors. Everything about the business says own it. Two things say wait: the price, and the fact that it doubles this book's AI bet.
