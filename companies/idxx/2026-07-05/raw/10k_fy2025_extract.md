## Company / Business Description

IDEXX Laboratories, Inc. (NASDAQ: IDXX), headquartered in Westbrook, Maine, is a global leader in
veterinary/pet healthcare diagnostics. Develops, manufactures, and markets diagnostic instruments,
consumables, reference laboratory services, and practice-management/veterinary software for veterinary
practices, plus water quality testing and livestock/poultry/dairy diagnostics.

## Reportable Segments (as filed)

1. **Companion Animal Group (CAG)** — the dominant segment (~92% of FY2025 revenue):
   - CAG Diagnostics recurring revenue: VetLab consumables (analyzer slides/cartridges/reagents),
     Reference Laboratory Services (IDEXX reference lab network), Rapid Assay products (SNAP tests)
   - CAG Diagnostics capital (instruments): analyzer placements (Catalyst, ProCyte, SediVue, inVue Dx, etc.)
   - Veterinary Software/Diagnostic Imaging/Services: practice management software (Cornerstone, ezyVet,
     IDEXX Neo), imaging systems, VetConnect PLUS connectivity platform
2. **Water** — testing/analysis products for water quality and safety (Colilert, Quanti-Tray, etc.)
3. **Livestock, Poultry and Dairy (LPD)** — diagnostic products/services for production animal health
