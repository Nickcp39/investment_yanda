## M&A: Clario Acquisition (closed 2026-03-24)
- **Purchase price: $8.875B cash** (industry-leading provider of endpoint data solutions for clinical trials — imaging/eCOA/cardiac safety data used in drug development)
- Additional payments: $125M deferred (payable Jan 2027) + up to $400M performance-based earnout (2026-2027)
- Rationale: expands clinical-trial data capabilities within Laboratory Products & Biopharma Services (PPD/clinical research) segment — directly synergistic with BIOSECURE-Act-driven CRO reshoring tailwind (see facts.md)

## Divestiture: Microbiology business to Astorg

## BIOSECURE Act (signed into law Dec 2025)
- Prohibits US government agencies from contracting with designated "biotechnology companies of concern" — primarily targets Chinese CRO/CDMO firms (WuXi AppTec, WuXi Biologics named in general coverage)
- Casper (at JPM Healthcare Conference, Jan 2026) cited BIOSECURE + Catalent/Novo deal as growth drivers; "very positive" outlook on sterile/CDMO demand
- TMO's PPD clinical-research and pharma-services (CDMO) businesses positioned as primary beneficiaries — customers reshoring work away from Chinese CRO/CDMO providers
- CDMO rates reported expected to rise 5-10% annually as capacity tightens (industry-general commentary, not TMO-specific confirmed figure)
