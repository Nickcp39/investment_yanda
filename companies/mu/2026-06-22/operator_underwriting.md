### Team life-arc grade: ~4/5 (qualified-positive, cycle-tested)

A founder-grade memory CEO, a cohesive core that came together in 2017 and has run through a full cycle
(FY23 trough → FY26 supercycle), a deep technical bench, and clean governance. Mehrotra's ~28-yr non-fractious
SanDisk co-founder partnership (with Harari) is evidence he sustains long senior relationships.

### Dark-arc (required — the bear case on the people, now partly LIVE)

1. **Cycle-long bias (the big one, now LIVE):** a ~40-yr memory builder, and a SanDisk/WD-bred core, is
   structurally inclined to stay *long* the technology and *build through* gluts. The person least able to
   conclude "this up-cycle is ASP-flattered, slow the capex" is the one who believes in memory most. At a
   cycle peak with the stock at an ATH, this is the most important risk to watch — over-building DRAM/HBM
   capacity into peak demand is the classic memory-CEO error.
2. **Optimism-at-the-top:** the "record / sustained strength of our business" framing (Q2 FY26 release) is
   confident at what may be a cycle peak. Track whether management starts hedging on cycle position.
3. **Capital intensity:** ~$5B/qtr capex; in a down-cycle this turns FCF negative fast (FY23 precedent).
4. **Age / succession:** ~68, no publicly mapped CEO successor on a 5–10-yr bet.

### Verdict → durability ceiling

- **Team grade 4/5 RAISES conviction within the cyclical band** (this is a serious, vindicated, cohesive
  team — take its execution seriously) **but does NOT lift the structural ceiling above the cyclical band**
  per METHODOLOGY. Operator quality does not convert commodity memory into a 5–10-yr secular compounder; only
  durable HBM differentiation could, and that remains the open thesis question. **No governance discount on
  structure** (clean one-share-one-vote); a modest cyclicality/key-man-age caution on substance.
- **Duan IC lens ("管理层是否靠谱"):** 人非常靠谱 —— 创始人级行业老兵、治理干净、已穿越完整周期的深厚团队,而且当年的 HBM/AI 押注现在被业绩证明是对的。约束从来不在人,而在生意的周期性、资本密集度,以及现在最关键的:**在周期顶部、股价新高时,这个"信存储、爱建产能"的团队能否克制住不在高点过度扩产**。人对,但这是一个周期顶部的资本纪律考验,尚未验证。
