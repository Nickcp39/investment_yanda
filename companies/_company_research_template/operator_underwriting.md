## Temperament Checklist

- Does management communicate candidly?
- Do incentives reward per-share value or empire building?
- Do leaders act quickly when wrong?
- Is the culture product-led, finance-led, sales-led, or political?
- Would we trust this team with 10 years of retained earnings?

## Guardrail

Do not turn personality analysis into vibes. Use letters, transcripts, compensation design, capital allocation decisions, employee patterns, and crisis behavior as evidence.
