## Questions

- Can competitors buy around the bottleneck?
- Is the bottleneck temporary or structural?
- Does the company own the bottleneck or merely depend on it?
- Does solving the bottleneck improve economics or destroy returns through capex?

## Risk

A bottleneck is not automatically a moat. It can also be a cost sink, capacity ceiling, or regulatory exposure.
