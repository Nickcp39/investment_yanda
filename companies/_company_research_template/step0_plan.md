## Research Question

What exact decision should this research support?

```text
Is <company> a business we would want to own for 10 years?
At what price would the expected return and margin of safety be attractive?
What would prove us wrong?
```

## Non-Goals

- Do not make a short-term earnings trade call.
- Do not convert a KOL story into a thesis.
- Do not write a final memo before the evidence layer is built.
