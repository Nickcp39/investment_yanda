## Quality Questions

- Is revenue recurring, transactional, cyclical, or one-time?
- Does growth require rising capital intensity?
- Are margins expanding because of scale, accounting, pricing, or temporary utilization?
- Is SBC a real economic cost here?
- Are buybacks reducing share count or just offsetting dilution?
- Is reported FCF overstating owner earnings?

## Red Flags

- Revenue grows but owner earnings per share stagnate.
- Capex rises faster than durable revenue.
- SBC grows faster than operating income.
- Segment reporting hides deteriorating economics.
