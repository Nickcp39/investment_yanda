## Kill / three-state triggers

- **GREEN (hold/add):** procedures hold ~14-15%, I&A-per-procedure stable, placements strong, margins in guide;
  price < ~$412.
- **YELLOW (re-underwrite / pause adds):** any one of — procedure growth toward ~10%, I&A-per-procedure softening,
  placement growth stalling, or Hugo taking visible urology share; or price > ~$520.
- **RED (avoid/trim):** procedure growth < ~10% AND I&A-per-procedure falling AND placements negative (annuity
  erosion confirmed); or a value-destructive large acquisition; or price > ~$600 without earnings step-up.

## Entry triggers (the STARTER → ADD/CORE ladder)

- **STARTER now** (~$426) — thin-but-positive base IRR on a near-monopoly diversifier.
- **Add toward Confirmed (~8-12% cap)** at <= ~$412 AND a clean Q2 confirming the annuity holds.
- **CORE-size discipline** only near <= ~$364 (conservative-exit MOS) — a larger position needs the deeper margin.
