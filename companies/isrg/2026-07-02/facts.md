## INTERPRETATION

- ISRG is a surgical-robotics **near-monopoly** on a **razor-and-blade** model: a large, growing installed base (**11,106 da Vinci + 954 Ion**) pulls a **~75-76% recurring** stream of high-margin instruments/accessories + service; Systems (~25%) is the razor. Deep switching costs (surgeon training + hospital workflow + capital sunk). Business quality +2.
- **Diversification:** ISRG is medtech / surgical-robotics — **NOT an AI-capex/liquidity factor**. It genuinely diversifies the user's BTC/GOOGL/NBIS single-factor book (its only AI-adjacent thread is speculative OpenAI-robotics fear, not a real linkage). This is a real second axis HWM shares but at a very different price setup.
- After a ~30% de-rate, the price now offers a **positive base-case 5y IRR (~+9%/yr)** that roughly clears an 8% hurdle — unlike HWM/GEV (negative base). The margin of safety is thin but real (see valuation.md).
