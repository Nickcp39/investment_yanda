## Block: competition (primary/FDA)

- Medtronic Hugo RAS: FDA-cleared for US urology 2025-12-03 (prostatectomy, nephrectomy, cystectomy; ~230k US surgeries/yr). Expand URO IDE (137 pts) met primary efficacy & safety endpoints (AUA 2025). GYN + general-surgery submissions to follow; US rollout beginning. Ex-US in >30 countries.
- J&J Ottava: De Novo request submitted to FDA ~2026-01-07 (general surgery, upper abdomen). IDE approved Nov 2024; FORTE bariatric study met primary endpoints. No FDA decision/launch date guided.
- CMR Surgical Versius Plus: FDA 510(k) cholecystectomy (Dec 2025); gyn submission Apr 2026. Distalmotion Dexter: 3rd FDA clearance (hysterectomy) Oct 2025. Both narrow single-specialty US footholds.
- China: not a separate reported segment. US = 68% of FY2025 revenue, ex-US = 32%. Domestic Chinese robots surpassed da Vinci in Chinese public tenders for the first time in 2025 (~52% of awarded volume; Edge Medical, MicroPort Toumai). China DRG reimbursement overhaul unresolved until 2027. China-specific % of revenue OPEN (~mid-single-digit).
