## Block A — FY2025 10-K / results PR [S001/S002/S007]
- Two reportable segments (reorganized): **Automation Enabling Technologies** and **Medical Solutions**.
- AET FY2025 revenue $500.8M (2024 $490.6M), GM 47.8%, op profit $114.5M. "Revenue increased $10.2M / 2.1% vs 2024, primarily due to a **$30.3M increase in robotics and automation products**, partially offset by a **$20.0M decline in precision manufacturing products**."
- Medical Solutions FY2025 revenue $479.8M (2024 $458.6M), GM 41.6%, op profit $51.2M. "Increased $21.1M / 4.6%, primarily driven by a **$33.7M increase in advanced surgery products**, partially offset by a **$12.6M decline in precision medicine products** (lower demand)."
- End markets: "the medical market accounted for approximately **53%** of our revenue"; "the advanced industrial market accounted for approximately **47%**."
- AET product families: laser beam steering/scanning, laser sources, robotic and precision motion, robotic end-of-arm tooling, air-bearing spindles.
- Applications listed: "robotic surgery, warehouse automation, **humanoids**, semiconductor, agricultural, metrology, satellite communications, medical devices, laboratory and diagnostics."
- FY2025: revenue $980.6M (+3.3%); GAAP NI $53.8M (EPS $1.47); adj EPS $3.29; adj EBITDA $221.0M; OCF $64.1M; FCF $54.0M.
- Balance sheet 12/31/25: cash $380.9M; total debt $250.8M (gross $259.6M); **net cash ~$121.3M**. Nov-2025 tangible equity units → ~$613.1M net proceeds; $1.0B senior credit facility (Jun-2030).
- Shares: 35,683,769 common outstanding as of Feb 16 2026 (10-K cover).

## Block B — Q1 2026 earnings call / recap [S003/S004/S011]
- Q1 2026 revenue $257.7M (+10% reported, +3% organic). Beat estimates.
- Bookings +37% YoY; book-to-bill 1.10 (company), 1.15 (AET). Management: "primarily a genuine step-up in demand… typical two-quarter lag to revenue," but bookings **included some full-year orders placed by customers in Q1**.
- **Robotics & Automation bookings +50% YoY; revenue +7%** ("high single-digit").
- **NVIDIA Holoscan**: "We're the only servo drive manufacturer that got selected" for the Holoscan AI Systems Inspection Lab (safety validation for humanoids/warehouse/precision robotics). "Still in a prototype phase," **"more meaningful" in 2027**.
- Advanced Surgery growth driven by minimally invasive + robotic surgery, plus a "rapidly scaling medical **consumables** franchise that now represents **15% of total revenue**."
- Guidance: FY2026 revenue $1.040–1.055B; adj EBITDA $245–250M; Q2 revenue $259–264M (6–8% organic).
