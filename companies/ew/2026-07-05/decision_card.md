## Career-Relevant Finding (China TAVR Competition)

Edwards entered China **late (2020)** — 1-3 years after domestic players **Venus Medtech, JC Medical, and CardioFlow Medtech** received their own approvals (2017-2019). Result: Chinese domestic players collectively hold **~80% of China's TAVR market** vs. Edwards' **~60% GLOBAL** share — an almost exact inversion. Six approved China TAVR players total: Venus Medtech, MicroPort CardioFlow, JC Medical, **Peijia Medical**, Edwards, Medtronic. Chinese domestic products run **~40% cheaper** and increasingly cite their own long-term (10-year) clinical data (Venus Medtech claim). Revenue-scale gap: Edwards' H1 2025 revenue (~$2.94B) is **~83x** the largest Chinese domestic competitor (Balance Medical, ~$35M). Unlike GEHC's **confirmed, already-declining** China revenue (-15% YoY), Edwards' China position is a **persistently low starting share** in a market still small today but forecast to grow ~55.5%/yr — an ongoing, unresolved battle, not a settled loss. **Edwards' own China-specific revenue is not disclosed anywhere found this run** — the single highest-priority research gap for next refresh.
