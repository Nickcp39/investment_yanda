# HWM Business Model (M2 / M3 input)

As-of: 2026-06-22 | Pipeline: lean-6module-v1.1

## One sentence

Howmet makes the scarce, certified, high-temperature/high-stress metal parts that jet engines, airframes, defense platforms, industrial gas turbines, and heavy trucks cannot fly or run without — and earns 30%+ segment EBITDA because those parts are qualification-gated and switching is nearly impossible.

## Revenue engines (4 segments; Q1 2026)

| Segment | Q1 rev | % of rev | Seg EBITDA margin | What it is | Why it earns |
|---|---:|---:|---:|---|---|
| **Engine Products** | $1,253M | ~54% | **36.6%** | Investment-cast single-crystal superalloy turbine airfoils/blades/vanes for commercial + defense jet engines and IGT | The crown jewel: multi-year qualification, sole/dual-source, decades-long spares pull off the installed base |
| **Fastening Systems** | $471M | ~20% | 31.8% | Aerospace/industrial fasteners (engineered, certified) | Spec-in, low cost-per-part / high cost-of-failure, sticky |
| **Engineered Structures** | $294M | ~13% | 22.4% | Titanium airframe & engine structures | Capital-intensive Ti forming; lumpier, lower margin |
| **Forged Wheels** | $295M | ~13% | 30.5% | Forged aluminum wheels for heavy commercial trucks | #1 share; cyclical with truck builds (currently -11% volume) |

## Demand drivers

1. **Commercial-aero up-cycle** — Boeing/Airbus narrow- and wide-body build-rate recovery off record backlogs (+20% commercial aero in Q1).
2. **Engine SPARES (aftermarket)** — high-margin, recurring; aging global fleet + high utilization pulls replacement airfoils. Management: "engine spares needs continue to increase." This is the annuity layer.
3. **Defense** — fighter/rotorcraft engines and structures (+10%).
4. **Gas turbines / data-center power** — IGT airfoils riding the power build-out (+39%); the only (modest) AI-adjacent thread.
5. **Heavy trucks** — Forged Wheels, currently a cyclical drag (-11% volumes).

## 10-year test (would you own it for a decade?)

- **For the business: yes.** Bottleneck moat, recurring spares, fortress balance sheet, elite allocator, secular aero growth.
- **At this price: no.** 65x trailing / 57x forward earnings prices the entire up-cycle + a permanent premium multiple; 5y base IRR is negative (see valuation.md).
