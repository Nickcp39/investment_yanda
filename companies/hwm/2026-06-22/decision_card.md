## The one-paragraph thesis

HWM is a top-tier pure-play on the scarce, high-spec nodes of the jet-engine supply chain — investment-cast single-crystal superalloy turbine airfoils (Engine Products, 36.6% segment EBITDA), aerospace fasteners, and engineered structures — riding a genuine multi-year commercial-aero build-rate up-cycle plus a high-margin, recurring engine-**spares** annuity. Q1 2026 was a clean beat-and-raise (revenue +19%, adj EBITDA margin 32.0% +320bps, FCF strong), the balance sheet is a fortress (~0.7x net debt, A- rated), and the operator (John Plant) is an elite capital allocator. Everything about the business says own it. The price says wait.

## Why WATCH, not STARTER

At **$280.36** the stock is **65x trailing / 57x forward earnings, ~37x EV/EBITDA** — near an all-time high after a +65%-off-low run. The 5-year IRR is **negative in the base case (~-1.6%/yr)** and only **+4.2%/yr in the bull case** — all three scenarios fail an 8% hurdle. Multiple compression (a de-rate from 57x toward a still-premium 30x) is the dominant downside and is ~-45% on its own. There is **zero margin of safety**.
