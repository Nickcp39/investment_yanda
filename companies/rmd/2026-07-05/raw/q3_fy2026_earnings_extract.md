## GLP-1 data presented on earnings call (career-relevant)
- Study 1 (1.7M de-identified patient records): PAP+GLP-1 patients show +5.1% higher 2-year resupply rate, +6.2% higher 3-year resupply rate vs PAP-only

- 2.1M+ patients currently identified as using both GLP-1 and ResMed CPAP therapy concurrently
- CEO framing: GLP-1 is "a once-in-a-generation demand gen opportunity for ResMed"
