# 2026 Market Map

## Purpose

Define the first research thread for this workspace: what to analyze, what to
produce, and where the boundary sits between research and execution.

## Analysis Universe

- U.S. equities
- BTC and crypto liquidity cycles
- AI infrastructure, semiconductors, cloud, and software
- Macro drivers: rates, liquidity, inflation, labor, fiscal impulse, and dollar
- Personal portfolio decisions that need a written research trail

## Output Shape

- Research memos
- Company notes
- Sector maps
- Macro regime notes
- Valuation tables
- Dashboard prototypes
- Data collection scripts
- Investment decision logs

## Boundary

This workspace is for analysis only. It should not place trades or automate
execution. A future bot or agent can consume selected outputs from this repo,
but execution should remain a separate layer.

## Current Questions

- Which markets or sectors deserve recurring coverage?
- Which companies need full research notes first?
- Which macro indicators should be tracked weekly?
- Which data sources are reliable enough to automate?
- What would make an idea investable rather than merely interesting?

## Operating Principles

- Prefer primary sources when available.
- Keep dates on time-sensitive claims.
- Distinguish facts from estimates and judgment calls.
- Write down the base case, upside case, downside case, and kill criteria.
- Do not let dashboards replace thesis quality.
