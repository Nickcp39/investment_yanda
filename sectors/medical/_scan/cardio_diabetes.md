## SUB-SECTOR THEMES (the big picture)

1. **PFA share war has flipped — Medtronic overtook Boston Scientific.** Pulsed-field ablation is the hottest cardiac story. BSX held ~100% of PFA spend in 2023; by early 2026 that fell to **~41%**, while **Medtronic surged to ~48%**, J&J (Varipulse) ~11%, Abbott (Volt, launched Jan 2026) ~1%. The overall PFA market is ~$2.0B in 2026 growing ~26% CAGR. The narrative moved from "BSX owns AFib" to "BSX must defend share against a resurgent MDT." (Source: Guidepoint/QSight, MedTech Dive, 2026)

2. **Structural heart is broadening beyond TAVR into AR + heart failure + mitral/tricuspid.** TAVR growth re-accelerated in 2026 (EW raised TAVR guide to 7–9%), but the real expansion is into previously untreated pools: aortic regurgitation (EW's JenaValve Trilogy), heart-failure monitoring (EW's Endotronix), and TMTT replacement (SAPIEN M3, EVOQUE, PASCAL) growing ~40%+. This is a multi-year TAM-expansion theme.

3. **CGM/pump demand is compounding, and GLP-1 is a NET TAILWIND (not the feared headwind).** The 2024–25 fear that GLP-1 drugs would shrink diabetes-device demand has largely reversed: consensus now views GLP-1 as neutral-to-positive for CGM (Dexcom pushing "GLP-1 users should also wear a CGM" via OTC Stelo) and only minor risk to insulin pumps (core T1D untouched). Insulet (+34% rev) and Dexcom (+15%) are the growth engines of the whole sub-sector; Abbott Libre is the scaled incumbent temporarily slowed by a tender-timing issue.

- **Guidance (RAISED):** FY26 sales growth to **9–11%** (from 8–10%); total sales $6.5–6.9B; TAVR $4.7–5.0B; TMTT $740–780M; surgical mid-single-digit. Adj EPS midpoint raised to **$2.95–3.05**.
- **Strategy / M&A / pipeline:** Portfolio expansion via **JenaValve** (Trilogy — first therapy for aortic regurgitation, FDA approval expected/around late-2025→2026) and **Endotronix** (implantable heart-failure monitor) — ~$1.2B + up to $445M milestones (announced Jul-2024, integrating through 2026). Newly approved SAPIEN M3 for mitral replacement is a 2026 launch driver. AI specifics `[unknown]` from this scan.
- **Notable now:** TMTT inflection (~42% growth) + entry into aortic regurgitation and heart-failure — Edwards building the second and third legs beyond TAVR.
- **Valuation (approx, UNVERIFIED):** Forward P/E **~27.6x** (as of ~2026-04-28) — a clear premium to peers (industry median ~19.8x). Premium reflects pure-play structural-heart growth + margin.
- **Nick-edge fit:** Structural-heart procedural imaging (echo/CT sizing, fusion guidance) is imaging-heavy; AR/HF monitoring adds a data/algorithm layer — adjacent to imaging+DL, though EW itself is device-first.

### BSX — Boston Scientific (PFA leader on defense)
- **Latest reported (Q1 2026):** Revenue **$5.20B, +11.6% YoY**; adj EPS **$0.80** (in-line). (Source: PRNewswire / Investing.com, 2026)
- **Guidance (CUT):** FY26 revenue growth cut to **6.5–8.5%** (from 10.5–11.5%) — guidance reflects **larger-than-expected PFA share erosion**. Global EP still expected ~+10% (US mid-single-digit, intl +20%).
- **Strategy / pipeline:** Defending Farapulse franchise — **FARAPOINT** (focal PFA catheter) running ahead of expectations; OPAL mapping + FARAWAVE NAV. Watchman (LAAC) US sales flattening = second soft spot.
- **Notable now:** The one thing moving the story is **PFA share loss** — from ~100% (2023) to ~41% (early 2026) as MDT/JNJ/ABT entered. Guidance cut despite a Q1 top/bottom beat is the tell.
- **Valuation (approx, UNVERIFIED):** One data source showed forward P/E ~12.8x — **this looks implausibly low for BSX** (historically ~30x+); treat as a data error / flag for verification.
- **Nick-edge fit:** EP relies on 3D electroanatomic mapping (OPAL) — real-time cardiac imaging + signal ML; strong AI-in-medtech adjacency.

## CANDIDATES WORTH A FULL PIPELINE DOSSIER (and why)

1. **MDT (Medtronic)** — the PFA share-taker with re-accelerating growth (best FY rev growth in a decade), CAS +78%, PFA +145%, yet a compressed ~13x forward P/E. Classic "improving fundamentals not yet in the multiple." Nick-edge: Affera/Sphere-9 = mapping+ablation imaging/ML. **Strong candidate.**

2. **PODD (Insulet)** — cleanest secular grower in the sub-sector (+34% rev, +59% intl, raised guide), GLP-1 fear now debunked, T2D closed-loop optionality (EVOLVE→2028). Pure execution/growth story. Nick-edge: closed-loop control ML (moderate). **Strong candidate.**

3. **EW (Edwards)** — highest-quality structural-heart pure-play with a re-accelerating TAVR + new AR/HF/mitral legs, but richest valuation (~27x) — dossier should stress-test whether the premium is earned vs. priced-in. Nick-edge: structural-heart imaging is the closest imaging fit in the group. **Candidate (valuation-gated).**

*Watch, not first-tier dossier:* **BSX** (guidance cut on PFA share loss — interesting as a possible over-punished contrarian, but the story is deteriorating, so it needs a "why it stops losing share" thesis first). **DXCM/ABT** = solid but either overhang (DXCM FDA letter) or diluted signal (ABT diversified + Exact Sciences).
