## 1) China revenue: % + absolute + 3–5yr trend + segment detail

- **Not separately disclosed.** ISRG 10-K breaks revenue only into US (68% of 2025 rev) vs OUS (32%). FY2025 total revenue **~$10.06B, +21% YoY** ([10-K FY2025](https://www.sec.gov/Archives/edgar/data/1035267/000103526726000010/isrg-20251231.htm)).
- **China est.:** ~5–7% of total, i.e. **~$0.5–0.7B (est., OPEN — no primary line item)**. China is a top-3 OUS market alongside Japan/Europe; ISRG has treated "nearly 1 million patients" in China over 20+ yrs ([China Daily 2022](https://www.chinadaily.com.cn/a/202208/08/WS62f0840da310fd2b29e70e61.html)).
- **Installed base (China-only): OPEN/est.** Global year-end 2025 base >12,100 systems (>11,100 da Vinci + 995 Ion). China est. ~350–450 da Vinci (est.). Da Vinci Xi assembled locally via the Intuitive-Fosun JV; **>80% of Xi installed in China in 2025 were locally made** ([search synthesis; ISRG/Fosun disclosures]).
- **3–5yr trend — MIXED→turning DOWN on capital, still UP on procedures:**
  - *Procedures:* global da Vinci procedures **+18% in 2025** to >3.1M (US +15%, intl +23%). China contributes to the intl growth engine — the razor-blade annuity is intact.
  - *Capital placements (the China stress point):* mgmt repeatedly called China **"constrained and competitive"** through 2025; Q3'25 flagged China as a drag on capital placements. Provincial tenders increasingly favor local suppliers → **ISRG win-ratio falling** ([ISRG Q3'25 call synthesis](https://www.sec.gov/Archives/edgar/data/1035267/000103526725000206/q325ex-991earningsrelease.htm)).
- **Net:** China revenue mix likely **flat-to-declining as a share** while global grows — the capital line is where China hurts; the installed-base annuity is stickier.

## 2) VBP / 集采 exposure

- **Yes, and 2025 was the inflection.** China ran multi-tier centralized procurement of 腔镜手术机器人 in 2025 (国家卫健委 + 军队 + 地方 + 联盟); 集采中标台数占比已超 20% of the market ([新浪财经](https://finance.sina.com.cn/stock/relnews/hk/2025-05-08/doc-inevwchs8468970.shtml); [OFweek 2025-12](https://m.ofweek.com/medical/2025-12/ART-11111-8120-30675767.html)).
- **Repricing magnitude — severe on the capital box:** da Vinci per-system price fell from **RMB 25–30M → ~RMB 17M (−32% to −40%)**; the locally-made IS4000CN median tender ~RMB 18M, with a **RMB 13M** national-health-commission win reported late-2025 ([新浪财经 1300万](https://cj.sina.com.cn/articles/view/5953740931/162dee083067037o06)). "国产优先" is explicit tender policy ([腾讯新闻 2025-05](https://news.qq.com/rain/a/20250525A05HAK00)).
- **Product lines hit:** the **capital system** (da Vinci Xi/5) is the VBP target. Instruments & accessories (I&A) also see intl price pressure per mgmt, but the per-procedure annuity is less exposed than the box.
- **Mgmt commentary:** tariff impact ~0.7% of rev (≈half US-China); non-GAAP GM guided **67–67.5% (2025) vs 69.1% (2024)** — China/intl pricing is part of that margin give-back. Mgmt frames China as a **regional headwind, not a thesis-breaker** ([Investing.com SWOT](https://www.investing.com/news/swot-analysis/intuitive-surgicals-swot-analysis-robust-procedure-growth-drives-stock-outlook-amid-tariff-challenges-93CH-4364180)).

## 3) Local competition — NAME them + share trend

**This is the headline. ISRG's China near-monopoly is visibly cracking.** 2025 first-3-quarters 腔镜手术机器人 unit-sales share ([OFweek](https://m.ofweek.com/medical/2025-12/ART-11111-8120-30675767.html)):

| Rank | Player | 2025 9M share | Note |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | **达芬奇 / da Vinci (ISRG)** | **43.2%** | down from ~90%+ historically — a structural break |
| 2 | **精锋 / Edge Medical (MP1000)** | 9.31% | multi-port, >2,500 cases, 80+ hospitals/26 provinces; Tencent/ME capital, HK IPO track |
| 3 | **思哲睿 / Sagebot (康多 KangDuo)** | 7.71% | Harbin-based; A-share listed |
| — | **微创图迈 / MicroPort Toumai** | (in field) | global installs >100 units, orders >160 (40+ countries), >15,000 cases; leading 出海 name; **远程/telesurgery** differentiator |
| — | **术锐 / Surgerii (single-port)** | (in field) | NMPA expanded 2025-05, CE (MDR) 2025-08; >2,000 cases, #2 globally in single-port; all-ages/pediatric |

- **Share trend: clearly DOWN for ISRG on units.** From effective monopoly to ~43% of new placements in ~3 years. Domestic packaged bids (e.g. 江西九江: 精锋 MP1000 + 天智航 packaged RMB 16.1M) undercut hard.
- **Caveat (real replacement vs low-end 分流):** domestics win on **price + 国产优先 policy + capital-budget-constrained hospitals**. da Vinci still dominates **complex/high-volume academic centers, clinical-evidence depth (25yr), surgeon-training moat, and the installed-base annuity**. So it is **partial displacement at the capital margin, not yet annuity replacement** — but Toumai's telesurgery and Surgerii's single-port are pushing into genuine capability, not just cheap knockoffs. Trend arrow points to continued share erosion.

## 4) Localization / strategy — EXPANDING vs DIVESTING

- **EXPANDING, not divesting** (opposite of the GEHC China-exit narrative). Intuitive-Fosun JV (est. 2017, direct ops from 2019) locally assembles da Vinci Xi; **>80% of China Xi locally made in 2025**. ISRG committed a **~$100M China investment** (Shanghai/Fosun ecosystem) to expand RAS + training ([China Daily 2022](https://www.chinadaily.com.cn/a/202208/08/WS62f0840da310fd2b29e70e61.html)).
- **Rationale:** localization is the defense — locally-made IS4000CN qualifies for "国产" tender treatment and cost parity, blunting VBP. This is a **fight-to-stay** posture, not a retreat. No credible divestiture signal (contrast: GEHC reportedly exploring China separation).

## 6) NET China thesis (one line)

**MIXED, tilting to DISPLACEMENT+HEADWIND.** China is simultaneously a **procedure tailwind** (RAS penetration still early, annuity growing) and a **capital-side headwind** (VBP −32–40% price cuts) plus a **structural国产替代 displacement** (ISRG new-placement share ~90%→43% in ~3yr, with 微创图迈/精锋/术锐/思哲睿 all scaling). Evidence: 2025 9M unit share 43.2%; da Vinci system price to RMB 13–17M; "国产优先" tenders; ISRG's own "constrained/competitive China" language.

## 7) Investment implication — tie to existing dossier

- The 2026-07-02 dossier rated ISRG **STARTER**, with China only as **kill-criterion K-E**. This brief says **K-E is live and worsening** — China is the clearest structural crack in the moat, alongside Hugo/Ottava in the US.
- **China is a MINUS, and a partial swing factor — but not the primary swing factor.** The dossier's swing factors remain (a) instrument-life I&A cannibalization and (b) US exit multiple / Hugo placement share.
